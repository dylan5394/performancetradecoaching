How long does it take?

Learning to control your emotions, to understand and accept risk, develop an edge, and therefore become independent in your profitable trading of the financial markets can take time and patience. It is a process that once understood and grasped, will accelerate your ability to create a healthy environment for trading and your method will become your own, a part of you and how you do things. Sometimes it is just some simple tweaking and sometimes it can take months or years depending on where you are in the process.

One thing is for sure, it is not an over night learning curve, it takes time, patience, and a burning desire to get to a place where it is what you do well.
