THE STORY

"My Story is Your Story"

Through years of trading and money spent on services and systems, I came to a place where I knew the in and outs of my trading system but still was not profitable. It was becoming clear to me that the system was one part of the equation, but that I was the other part. I knew at that point that it was me that was trading the system and that I was the thing that needed to change in order for me to reach my dream of profitable trading. I would lose money and blame the system, I would move to another system and would again lose money, this went on for me for many years until I started to teach and coach and in doing so and hearing the same stories from people like me that wanted financial freedom, a nest egg, a retirement, or to get out of the cubicle they spent their day in that trading successfully, making consistent profit year over year is an inside job, it takes knowing who you are, what makes you tic, facing your fears, controlling your emotions-these things are not taught when you sign up for services or systems-they are things that can be taught, must be taught and learned over and over until you understand yourself. You have to learn to own your trading and your system and when you and your trade system become one and the same, profitability will be in your reach, goals will be met, dreams will be realized. Performance Trade Coaching is simple. It offers mentoring online on an hourly basis that can be used as needed. No pre loading just pay as you go and as you need. We can be another set of eyes, we can hold you to accountability, we can help you in any way to reach your dreams, we are simply here for you because that is what we needed for ourselves and took years to get on our own.

If my story is your story Performance Trade Coaching can help you.
